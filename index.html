Game Instructions

Welcome to the Jungle

You are an Adventurer out in the Jungle. You found that this jungle is full of surprises, and you need to play a game for your survival.

In the game, you can collect fruits and flowers which will also give you some points.
Fruits : +10 Points {🍋🍒🍍🍎🥕🍇🍉🍓🍐}
Flowers : +5 Points {🌻🌺🌹}

In the game, you have 5 lives(❤️) in total. If you miss any fruit or flower, you will loose a life.
Special Item : Collect hearts to increase your lives if you have lost any.
Heart (❤️) : +20 Points & +1❤️

Jungle being a Jungle!!! There are also dangers from which you need to save yourself.
Tap on Dangers : -20 Points {🎃🌵🐍🐯🦊🦁🐺}
